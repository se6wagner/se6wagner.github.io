CTA Coaching

The last step of the Salesforce Certified Technical Architect examination process - the board review - not only represents a big challenge even for the most experienced architects, it also has a very low pass rate amongst Salesforce partners (25% - as provided by SFDC during 'Seed-The partner workshop' in April 2013). Even though Salesforce provides a large set of reference material and a small set hypothetical case studies for practice, they do not provide much support beyond that. This coaching program has been tail-ordered to bridge this gap and to prepare candidates in the best possible way based on the following elements:

  • Candidate assessment
  • Knowledge transfer through demos and presentations
  • Theoretical exercises on board relevant topics
  • Practical exercises in form of hypothetical case studies
  • CTA clinic

It is especially valuable for independent contractors and freelancers or architects working for customers who don't have access to other Salesforce resources or fellow CTAs.

The program is structured as a series of remote sessions (up to 1.5 hrs) that are held over the course of multiple weeks. The sessions are generally on a 1-to-1 basis to focus on the individual students and deliver the most value for them.
